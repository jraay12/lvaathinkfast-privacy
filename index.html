Privacy Policy for LVAA ThinkFast

Last Updated: September 14, 2025

1Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you use our Student Application ("App"). This policy applies to all users of our App.

2Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Login Credentials: Username and password for authentication (credentials are managed through our existing web system)
  • Student Information: Student ID, name, course details, and academic year
  • Academic Data: Grades, schedules, scores, and academic progress information
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support

Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Time spent on different sections of the app
  • Error logs and crash reports for app improvement

3How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely for the following purposes:

Core Educational Services

  • Authentication: Verify your identity and provide secure access to your academic information
  • Academic Information Display: Show your class schedules, grades, scores, and academic progress
  • Educational Resources: Provide access to ebooks and learning materials relevant to your courses
  • Administrative Services: Generate gate passes and provide access to student handbooks
  • Communication: Send important academic notifications and updates

App Improvement

  • Analyze app performance and user experience
  • Fix technical issues and improve app functionality
  • Ensure app security and prevent unauthorized access

4Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: To provide educational services and improve app functionality
  • Consent: Where required by applicable law, we obtain your explicit consent
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with educational and regulatory requirements

5Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Educational Institution: With your school's authorized personnel for academic administration
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in app operation (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process

Third-Party Services

If we use third-party services, we ensure they:

  • Comply with applicable privacy laws
  • Implement appropriate security measures
  • Use data only for specified purposes outlined in this policy

6Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access your personal information stored in the app
  • Update Information: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

Consent Management

  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent at any time where consent is the legal basis
  • Personalized Content: Opt-out of personalized features if available

Account Management

  • Account Deregistration: Contact your educational institution to deactivate your account
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal and educational requirements)

7Data Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization protocols
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with appropriate safeguards
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and updates

Data Retention

  • Academic data is retained as required by educational policies and legal requirements
  • Usage data is retained for app improvement purposes for a reasonable period
  • Data is securely deleted when no longer necessary

8Children's Privacy

This app is designed for students and may be used by users under 18. We:

  • Collect minimal necessary information for educational purposes
  • Do not use student data for advertising or marketing
  • Comply with applicable children's privacy laws (COPPA, GDPR, etc.)
  • Require appropriate consent mechanisms as mandated by law

9Advertising and Marketing

No Advertising Use

  • We do not use your academic or personal data for advertising purposes
  • We do not share your information with advertisers
  • Student data is used exclusively for educational services

Open Advertising Identifier (OAID)

  • If OAID is used, it is only for permitted analytical purposes
  • OAID is not associated with personal identity information
  • Users can disable personalized ads in device settings
  • We respect user preferences for ad personalization

10International Data Transfers

If your data is transferred internationally, we ensure:

  • Adequate protection measures are in place
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws
  • Appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers

11Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements
  • Users will be notified of material changes through the app or other appropriate means
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

13Compliance Information

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable laws including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for European Union
  • Personal Information Protection Law for Chinese mainland
  • Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance for Hong Kong
  • Personal Data Protection Act for Singapore
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Other applicable local privacy laws
